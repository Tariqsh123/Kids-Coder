What is Kids Coder?
Kids Coder is a platform that introduces children to programming through fun and interactive activities.
What age group is Kids Coder for?
Kids Coder is designed for children aged 7 to 14, depending on their interest and reading level.
Do kids need prior coding experience?
No previous experience is needed. Our lessons are beginner-friendly and progress step by step.
What languages or tools are taught?
We focus on Scratch, Python, and basic HTML/CSS depending on age and progression.
Are classes online or in-person?
All classes are conducted online through live sessions and recorded tutorials.
Is there any certification?
Yes, children receive a digital certificate after completing each level of the course.
How do I enroll my child?
You can sign up on our website by filling out a short form and selecting a course plan.
